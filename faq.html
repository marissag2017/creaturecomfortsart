Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions we've recieved.

What is the processing time for orders?

Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days. During holidays or seasonal sales, processing times may be longer than usual.

Do you ship internationally?

At this moment we only sell within the United States. However, we plan to start shipping internationally in the future!

What is your return policy?

We are sometimes able to offer replacements for damaged products or items lost in the mail within 30 days of purchase. This does depend on the product as our seasonal stickers are not able to be replaced due to limited stock. Feel free to contact us for assistance with returns!

Do you do custom orders/commissions and if so how can I contact you about it?

It depends on our artist's schedule, but we do sometimes take custom bulk orders. Please reach out through our contact page. We'd love to hear your ideas!

Do you partner with other creators?

Yes! We are always open to collaborations/partnerships with like-minded creators!

Where do you get your stickers manufactured/printed?

We currently order our products from Sticker App.

Have a question not featured above?

Please reach out to us through our contact page or send us a DM on Instagram!
